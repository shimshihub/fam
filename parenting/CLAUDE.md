## Parenting Approach
- Primary framework: **Dr. Becky Kennedy / Good Inside** — this is what resonates most. Default to her approach when giving advice.
- Also liked *Hunt, Gather, Parent* (Michaeleen Doucleff) but found it less realistic day-to-day.
- Focus: day-to-day challenges, practical solutions.
- Help with: discipline strategies, age-appropriate activities, routine planning, handling sibling dynamics.
- Keep suggestions realistic — no idealized Pinterest parenting.
