Tax Forms Cheatsheet

What your tax preparer asked for (2024 tax year) & where to find each one

W-2 From employer

Wage and Tax Statement

What Your salary, federal/state taxes withheld, Social Security & Medicare. One per job, per person.

Where Ori: TriNet → login → Pay → Tax Documents → W-2. Shayna: her employer's payroll portal. Sent by end of Jan.

1099s From banks / brokerages / others

Non-salary income — various types, you may not have all of them

1099-INT — Interest from bank accounts. → Wells Fargo: Sign in → Statements & Documents → Tax Documents.

1099-DIV — Dividends from stocks/funds. → Your brokerage → Tax Documents section.

1099-B — Proceeds from selling stocks/crypto. → Same brokerage, often in a "Consolidated 1099."

1099-NEC — Freelance/contract income. → The company that paid you sends it.

1099-MISC — Other income (rent, royalties, prizes). → Whoever paid you sends it.

Tip: Just gather whatever 1099s you received. Check Wells Fargo + any brokerage accounts.

1095s From insurer or employer

Health Coverage Statement — California requires proof of coverage

1095-B — Proof you had health insurance (from insurer).

1095-C — Proof employer offered coverage. Usually get this one if insured through work.

Where Ori: TriNet → login → Benefits or Tax Documents section. Or check your health insurer's website.

Childcare Expenses You gather this

For the Child and Dependent Care Credit (up to $6,000 for 2 kids)

What Total paid in 2024 for daycare/preschool for Roni & Yonatan.

Where Ask each provider for a year-end statement. Need: provider name, address, Tax ID (EIN/SSN), total paid.

If you used a Dependent Care FSA, that info is on your W-2, Box 10.
